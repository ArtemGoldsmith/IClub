BlackBerry Stock Hits Key Support After Sell Rating

Ico Marketing

BlackBerry Ltd BBRY 9.15 +0.60% Microsoft Corp MSFT 73.05 +0.90% VMware Inc VMW 93.42 0.00% Citrix Systems Inc CTXS 77.35 -1.43% Qualcomm Inc QCOM 53.31 +1.17% Shares of BlackBerry Limited (BBRY BlackBerry Ltd BBRY 9.15 +0.60% ) fell 3.6% on Monday after Goldman Sachs resumed its coverage of the stock with a Sell rating and an $8.50 per share price target. Goldman Sachs analyst Gabriela Borges notes that Microsoft Corporation (MSFT Microsoft Corp MSFT 73.05 +0.90% ), VMware, Inc. (VMW VMware Inc VMW 93.42 0.00% ), Citrix Systems, Inc. (CTXS Citrix Systems Inc CTXS 77.35 -1.43% ) and other competitors are starting to bundle enterprise mobility management solutions, which could increase competitive pressure over the coming quarters. Other analysts are more confident in BlackBerry stock despite its first quarter revenue miss in late June. For example, TD analysts reiterated their Buy rating and increased their price target to $11.50 per share, saying that the software focus overlooks strong traction in other areas.
